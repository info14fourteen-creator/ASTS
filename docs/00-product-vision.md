# Product Vision

## Working Name

ASTS: Automated System for Tender Sales.

Russian positioning:

> AI-система управления тендерной продажей: от разбора закупки до сбора КП и расчета рентабельности.

## Core Problem

Companies do not only need to find tenders. They need to decide quickly whether to participate, extract requirements from messy documents, request prices from suppliers, calculate profitability, and keep every person moving before the submission deadline.

Existing tender search tools help find opportunities, but the operational work after discovery is still manual, slow, and error-prone.

## Target Users

- Small and medium suppliers that participate in government and commercial tenders.
- Companies with 1-10 tender/procurement/sales specialists.
- Owners who want tender revenue but cannot afford a large tender department.
- Tender agencies that manage many procedures for clients.

## First Customer Promise

ASTS reduces the first analysis of a tender from hours to minutes and gives the team a controlled workflow until the bid decision.

## Non-Promise

ASTS should not promise automatic tender wins. The system assists, highlights risks, prepares data, and controls tasks. Final legal and commercial decisions stay with responsible people.

## Differentiation

We do not start with a tender database. We start with the operational layer:

- AI document analysis.
- Decision card.
- Supplier quote collection.
- Margin calculation.
- Role-based tasks and deadline escalation.

Search integrations can be added later through Tenderland exports, zakupki.gov.ru links, commercial APIs, or partner data.
